About This Project

A deep dive into the data, model, and technology behind our restaurant rating predictor.

Project Overview

The Bengaluru Restaurant Rating Predictor is a machine learning project that predicts the Zomato rating of a restaurant in Bengaluru based on key attributes such as location, cuisine type, cost, service type, and more.

This tool helps restaurant owners, food enthusiasts, and data science learners understand the factors that influence restaurant ratings in one of India's most vibrant food cities.

The Dataset

We used the Zomato Bengaluru Restaurant Dataset, which contains information about 50,000+ restaurants across Bengaluru. Key features include:

Online Order — Whether the restaurant accepts online orders
Book Table — Whether table booking is available
Votes — Total number of user votes
Location — Specific neighborhood in Bengaluru
Restaurant Type — Casual Dining, Café, Quick Bites, etc.
Cuisines — Cuisine combinations served
Cost for Two — Approximate meal cost for two people
Service Type — Buffet, Delivery, Dine-out, etc.
City Area — Broader city zone designation

Random Forest Regression

The model uses Random Forest Regression, an ensemble learning method that constructs multiple decision trees during training and outputs the average prediction.

Robust

Resistant to overfitting due to ensemble averaging across hundreds of decision trees.

Accurate

Handles non-linear relationships and feature interactions without explicit specification.

Feature Handling

Works with both numerical and encoded categorical variables seamlessly.

Fast Inference

Once trained, predictions are generated in milliseconds — perfect for web apps.
